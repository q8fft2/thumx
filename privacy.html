Privacy Policy

YouTube Frame Extractor is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

YouTube Frame Extractor is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect any personal information from you
  • We do not track your browsing habits
  • We do not store your YouTube viewing history
  • We do not send any of your data to remote servers

Local Storage

We only store your settings (like preferred language and format settings) locally on your device using browser's localStorage. This information never leaves your device and is solely used to improve your experience with the application.

YouTube Data

When you use our service to extract frames from YouTube videos:

  • We use the YouTube API to display videos
  • We only process the video frame data that you explicitly request
  • All processing happens in your browser, on your device
  • No video content or user data is sent to our servers

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
